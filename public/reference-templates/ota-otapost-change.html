What's New

As part of our ongoing commitment to innovation, efficiency, and operational improvement, we are pleased to officially announce the transition of all participating OTA Virtual Credit Card (VCC) hotels to our enhanced OTA POST Audit Program, effective immediately.

What Is OTA POST?

OTA POST is our next-generation OTA Revenue Recovery solution designed to streamline and automate the recovery process through our proprietary VCC processing system. Under OTA POST:

  • VNP Solutions processes OTA-issued VCC payments directly through our secure system
  • Hotels continue to retain 85% of all recovered funds
  • VNP Solutions absorbs all associated credit card processing fees
  • VNP Solutions assumes responsibility for managing and mitigating potential chargeback exposure
  • The need for manual posting and follow-up at the property level is significantly reduced
  • Recovery operations become centralized, more efficient, and more transparent
Continuous Weekly Audit Process

OTA POST services will operate on a continuous weekly audit cycle across all participating properties. To allow each hotel adequate time to internally identify and resolve discrepancies independently, VNP Solutions will maintain a rolling 30-day (one-month) grace period prior to initiating each audit review.
